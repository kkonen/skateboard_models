# Summary

A simple case for use when prototyping with the Adafruit Feather boards on a desk. Keeps the device safe from any conductive stuff that may come into contact with the base. 

Note that there are no clips or screws to hold the device in place if you turn the case over - this just meant to be used on a desk. You could always put an elastic band around it! :-)

There are opening for the USB and power connectors and a wide open space for the Bluetooth / WiFi antennas. 

Requires no support material - 26 min print time on the Stratsys uPrint I use.
